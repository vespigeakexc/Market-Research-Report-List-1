<p><p>The biobased films market comprises various types, including PBAT films, PLA films, and PHB films. PBAT (polybutylene adipate terephthalate) films offer flexibility and biodegradability, making them suitable for packaging applications. PLA (polylactic acid) films are derived from renewable resources, providing a sustainable alternative for single-use items. PHB (polyhydroxybutyrate) films are known for their high biodegradability and mechanical strength, making them ideal for various applications. Other biobased films include alternatives like PHA and starch-based films, enhancing the market's diversity.</p></p>
