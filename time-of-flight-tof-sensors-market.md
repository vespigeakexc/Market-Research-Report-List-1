<p><p>Time-of-Flight (ToF) sensors market includes three main types: RF-modulated light sources, which utilize radio frequency modulation for precise distance measurement; range-gated imagers that capture images within specific time frames to enhance depth perception and reduce ambient light interference; and direct Time-Of-Flight imagers, which measure the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, offering high-resolution depth information. Each type leverages unique technologies for applications in areas like robotics, automotive, and augmented reality.</p></p>
