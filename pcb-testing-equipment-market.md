<p><ul><li>AOI Tester</li><li>Impedance Tester</li><li>Flying Probe Tester</li><li>In-Circuit Tester</li><li>Automated Visual Inspection Tester</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB testing equipment market encompasses various technologies essential for ensuring circuit board quality. AOI testers use cameras to visually inspect boards for defects, while impedance testers measure electrical resistance to ensure proper connectivity. Flying probe testers employ movable probes for quick testing of PCB nodes. In-circuit testers check individual components for faults during the assembly process. Automated visual inspection testers enhance visual checks with automation, and other types include functional testers and X-ray systems for advanced diagnostics.</p></p>
