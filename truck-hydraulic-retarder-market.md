<p><strong>The Truck Hydraulic Retarder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hydraulic Retarder</li><li>Tandem Hydraulic Retarder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck hydraulic retarder market comprises two main types: parallel hydraulic retarders and tandem hydraulic retarders. Parallel hydraulic retarders utilize a single hydraulic unit to provide braking force, enhancing vehicle control and safety during descents. In contrast, tandem hydraulic retarders consist of two hydraulic units working in conjunction, offering greater braking power and efficiency, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types improve truck performance, extending brake life and ensuring smoother operation in various driving conditions.</p></p>
