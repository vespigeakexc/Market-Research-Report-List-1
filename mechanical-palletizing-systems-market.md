<p><strong>The Mechanical Palletizing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Level</li><li>High Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical palletizing systems are categorized into low-level and high-level systems based on their design and operation. </p><p>Low-level palletizers operate at floor height, where products are stacked onto pallets without the need for elevation. This type is ideal for smaller packages and offers easy access for operators. </p><p>In contrast, high-level palletizers lift products to height before stacking, maximizing space and efficiency in storage and shipping. These systems are well-suited for larger items and high-volume operations, offering greater automation and throughput capabilities.</p></p>
