<p><p>Disposable Filtering Facepiece Respirators (FFRs) are designed to protect users from airborne particles. The market is categorized into three classes: N Class, P Class, and R Class. N Class respirators are not resistant to oil and filter out at least 95% of airborne particles. P Class respirators are oil-proof, offering similar filtration efficiency but are suitable for environments where oil particles are present. R Class respirators are moderately resistant to oil, providing flexibility between the two types for varied industrial applications.</p></p>
