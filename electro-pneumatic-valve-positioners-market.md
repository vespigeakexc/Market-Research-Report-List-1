<p><p>The electro-pneumatic valve positioners market is primarily divided into single acting and double acting types. Single acting electro-pneumatic valve positioners utilize a spring mechanism to return the valve to a resting position, requiring air pressure for movement in one direction. In contrast, double acting positioners use air pressure for both opening and closing the valve, providing more precise control. This differentiation allows industries to select appropriate positioners based on their specific operational needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
