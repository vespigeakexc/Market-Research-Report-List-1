<p><p>The Flexible Intermediate Bulk Container (FIBC) bags market consists of three main types: Type A, Type B, and Type C. Type A bags are non-conductive and are suitable for storing non-flammable materials. Type B bags are designed to prevent the accumulation of ignitable dust, offering a minimal level of electrostatic discharge protection. Type C bags, also known as conductive bags, are equipped with conductive threads to safely transport flammable materials by dissipating static electricity, ensuring safety during handling and storage.</p></p>
