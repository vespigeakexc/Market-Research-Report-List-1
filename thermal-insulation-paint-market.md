<p><ul><li>Barrier Type</li><li>Reflective Type</li><li>Radiation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal insulation paint market comprises three main types: barrier type, reflective type, and radiation type. Barrier type paints contain materials that limit heat transfer by providing a thermal barrier, effectively reducing heat loss or gain in buildings. Reflective type paints incorporate reflective additives that bounce back infrared radiation, keeping surfaces cooler. Radiation type paints use specialized materials to absorb and emit thermal radiation, enhancing energy efficiency. Together, these types offer effective solutions for energy conservation in various applications.</p></p>
