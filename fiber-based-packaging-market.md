<p><ul><li>Virgin Fiber</li><li>Recycled Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber-based packaging comprises two main market types: virgin fiber and recycled fiber. Virgin fiber is sourced from freshly harvested trees, offering excellent strength and quality, making it ideal for high-end packaging applications. Recycled fiber, on the other hand, is derived from post-consumer paper and cardboard products, promoting sustainability and reducing environmental impact. Both types serve distinct purposes; virgin fiber is often used for premium packaging, while recycled fiber caters to eco-conscious consumers and brands seeking cost-effective, sustainable solutions.</p></p>
