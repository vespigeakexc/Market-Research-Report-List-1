<p><p>The metal decorating inks market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Water-Based Inks**: These inks are environmentally friendly, low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and suitable for various metal substrates, providing good adhesion and vibrant colors.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Solvent-Based Inks**: Known for their durability and resistance to chemicals and weather, these inks are ideal for outdoor applications but have higher VOC content.</p><p>3. **UV-Cured Inks**: These inks are cured by ultraviolet light, offering rapid drying times and high-quality finishes, making them popular for high-speed production and intricate designs on metal surfaces.</p></p>
