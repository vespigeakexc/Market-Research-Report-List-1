<p><strong>The Gellan Gum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Acyl Gellan Gum</li><li>High Acyl Gellan Gum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gellan gum is categorized into two main types: low acyl and high acyl gellan gum. Low acyl gellan gum forms a firm gel structure and is primarily used in applications requiring strong gel stability, such as in desserts and sauces. In contrast, high acyl gellan gum creates a softer, more elastic texture, making it suitable for products like yogurt and bakery fillings. Both types find extensive use in the food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries due to their gelling and stabilizing properties.</p></p>
