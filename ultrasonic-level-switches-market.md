<p><p>Ultrasonic level switches are devices that use high-frequency sound waves to detect the level of liquids and solids in various applications. They operate by emitting ultrasonic pulses and measuring the time it takes for the echoes to return from the surface of the material being measured. Externally mounted switches are installed outside the tank or container, using ultrasonic waves to detect levels without direct contact with the liquid. Both types offer reliable performance in diverse industrial environments, enhancing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
