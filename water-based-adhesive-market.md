<p><p>The water-based adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: natural and synthetic adhesives. Natural adhesives are derived from renewable sources such as plant-based materials or animal by-products, making them eco-friendly and biodegradable. They are commonly used in applications like woodworking and packaging. Synthetic adhesives, on the other hand, are chemically manufactured and offer enhanced performance, durability, and versatility across various industries, including construction and automotive. Both types cater to specific applications based on environmental concerns and performance requirements.</p></p>
