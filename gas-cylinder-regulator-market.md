<p><ul><li>Single-Stage</li><li>Dual-Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas cylinder regulator market is primarily categorized into single-stage and dual-stage regulators. Single-stage regulators reduce high cylinder pressure to a lower usable level in one step, often used for simpler applications and where precise control is less critical. In contrast, dual-stage regulators manage pressure reduction in two stages, providing more stable output and more accurate control, making them ideal for sensitive applications and environments where consistent pressure is crucial. The choice between them depends on specific usage requirements.</p></p>
