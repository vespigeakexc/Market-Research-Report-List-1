<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave Lasers</li><li>Pulsed Solid State Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid-state laser market is primarily categorized into Continuous Wave (CW) lasers and Pulsed Solid State Lasers. CW lasers emit a constant beam of light, ideal for applications requiring stable output, such as materials processing and medical procedures. In contrast, Pulsed Solid State Lasers generate light in short bursts, offering high peak power for applications like laser marking and engraving. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, defense, and manufacturing, reflecting the versatile use of solid-state technology.</p></p>
