<p><p>Whole house filtration systems encompass various technologies to purify water across an entire home. Activated carbon systems effectively remove chlorine, odor, and impurities. UV technology employs ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses without chemicals. Chemical-based filters use substances like chlorine or potassium permanganate for disinfection and contaminant removal. Reverse osmosis systems utilize a semi-permeable membrane to filter out dissolved solids, heavy metals, and chemicals. Other systems may include sediment filters or ion exchange technologies, addressing diverse water quality needs.</p></p>
