The market is divided into two main types based on membrane configurations: homogeneous membranes and heterogeneous membranes. Homogeneous membranes offer uniform properties and typically provide higher ion selectivity, making them effective in specific applications. In contrast, heterogeneous membranes possess varied properties that enhance performance in broader scenarios, improving the efficiency of ion removal and treatment processes while addressing diverse water quality challenges.</p></p>
