<p><strong>The Medical Grade Fiber Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-woven Fabric</li><li>Woven Fabric</li><li>Knitted Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical grade fiber fabric market is categorized into three main types: non-woven, woven, and knitted fabrics. Non-woven fabrics are created by bonding fibers together, offering breathability and fluid resistance, making them ideal for surgical and disposable applications. Woven fabrics are constructed by interlacing threads, providing durability and strength, typically used in long-lasting medical garments. Knitted fabrics, formed by interlocking loops of yarn, offer stretch and comfort, making them suitable for specialized medical apparel and bandages. Each type serves distinct medical needs.</p></p>
