<p><p>The magnetic float liquid level switch market is primarily categorized into two types: top-mounted and side-mounted. Top-mounted types are installed at the top of tanks, detecting liquid levels through the float that moves up and down with the liquid. This design is beneficial for applications requiring high accuracy and minimal maintenance. Side-mounted types, however, are affixed to the tank's side and are ideal for space-saving solutions. Both types offer reliable and cost-effective methods for liquid level monitoring in various industries.</p></p>
