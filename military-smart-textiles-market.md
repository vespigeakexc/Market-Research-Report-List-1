<p><p>The military smart textiles market comprises three main types: passive smart textiles, active smart textiles, and ultra-smart textiles. Passive smart textiles react to environmental changes without external power, offering features like moisture-wicking and temperature regulation. Active smart textiles integrate electronic components to detect and respond to stimuli, enhancing functionality such as communication and monitoring. Ultra-smart textiles take this a step further by offering advanced capabilities like self-healing and adaptive camouflage, ultimately providing improved protection and performance for military personnel.</p></p>
