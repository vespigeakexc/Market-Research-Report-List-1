<p><ul><li>Breathable Waterproof Fabrics</li><li>Non-Breathable Waterproof Fabrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterproof fabrics for sports apparel are categorized into breathable and non-breathable types. Breathable waterproof fabrics allow moisture vapor to escape while keeping water out, ensuring comfort during high-intensity activities. These fabrics often utilize technologies like microporous membranes or coatings. Non-breathable waterproof fabrics, on the other hand, provide a solid barrier against water but can trap sweat and humidity, making them less suitable for vigorous activities. Both types cater to specific needs based on activity intensity and environmental conditions.</p></p>
