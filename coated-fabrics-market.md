<p><p>The coated fabrics market is categorized into three main types: polymer coated fabrics, rubber coated fabrics, and others. Polymer coated fabrics are treated with various polymers like PVC or PU for enhanced durability and water resistance, making them ideal for outdoor and industrial applications. Rubber coated fabrics provide excellent elasticity and abrasion resistance, often used in heavy-duty environments. The "others" segment encompasses fabrics coated with specialty materials, including silicone and fluoropolymers, catering to niche markets with unique requirements.</p></p>
