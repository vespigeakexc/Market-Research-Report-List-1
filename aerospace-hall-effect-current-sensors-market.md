<p><strong>The Aerospace Hall Effect Current Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Loop</li><li>Closed-Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerospace Hall Effect current sensors are utilized for precise current measurements in various aerospace applications. The market is divided into two main types: open-loop and closed-loop sensors. Open-loop sensors measure the magnetic field generated by the current directly but are less accurate and sensitive to external influences. Closed-loop sensors, on the other hand, offer enhanced accuracy by utilizing feedback mechanisms to compensate for variations, making them more reliable for critical aerospace applications, where precision is essential.</p></p>
