<p><p>The Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) device market is primarily categorized into two types: first-generation QKD and second-generation QKD. In contrast, second-generation QKD enhances performance through advanced techniques such as entanglement and advanced error-correction methods, enabling more robust security features and increased efficiency, making it suitable for broader applications in secure communication networks.</p></p>
