<p><p>The sedan wheel market can be categorized into three main types: casting, forging, and other processes. Casting involves pouring molten metal into molds to create intricate designs, offering cost-effectiveness but often resulting in heavier wheels. Forging, on the other hand, utilizes compressed metal to create lighter and stronger wheels, enhancing performance and durability. The "other" category includes alternative manufacturing methods like machining and 3D printing, which allow for customization and innovation in design, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
