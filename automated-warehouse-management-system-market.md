<p><p>The Automated Warehouse Management System market comprises three main types. Supply Chain Module Systems enhance warehouse management by linking with supply chain processes, providing better visibility and coordination across operations. Integrated Systems combine warehouse management with other business functions, such as inventory, procurement, and logistics, facilitating seamless data flow and improved overall efficiency. Each type serves distinct organizational needs, influencing deployment strategies and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
