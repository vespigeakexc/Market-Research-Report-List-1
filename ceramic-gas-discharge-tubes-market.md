<p><p>The Ceramic Gas Discharge Tubes market comprises two main types: Surface Mount Gas Tube Arresters and Through Hole Gas Discharge Tubes. Surface Mount Gas Tube Arresters are designed for direct mounting on printed circuit boards, offering compactness and ease of installation. In contrast, Through Hole Gas Discharge Tubes are inserted through drilled holes in the PCB, providing robust protection and enhanced thermal management. Both types are crucial for safeguarding electronic equipment from voltage spikes and surges, ensuring reliability and longevity.</p></p>
