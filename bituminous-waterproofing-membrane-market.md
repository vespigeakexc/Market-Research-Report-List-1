<p><p>The Bituminous Waterproofing Membrane market consists of various types, including APP modified, SBS modified, and self-adhesive membranes. APP modified membranes are thermoplastic and provide excellent UV resistance, making them ideal for roofs exposed to sunlight. SBS modified membranes offer flexibility and elasticity, suitable for varying temperatures and movement in structures. Self-adhesive membranes are easy to install, adhering directly to surfaces without additional adhesives, providing a quick and efficient solution for waterproofing applications in both commercial and residential sectors.</p></p>
