<p><p>The soda feldspar market is primarily divided into two segments: plagioclase feldspar and K-feldspar. K-feldspar, containing potassium, is favored in high-quality ceramics and glass applications for its ability to improve strength and thermal resistance. Both types are essential for various industries, highlighting their crucial role in material production and innovation.</p></p>
