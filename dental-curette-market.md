<p><p>The dental curette market is segmented into two main types: single head and double head dental curettes. Single head curettes feature a single, curved working end designed for precise scaling and root planing in periodontal therapy. They are ideal for accessing narrow areas in the mouth. Double head curettes, on the other hand, have two working ends, allowing for versatility and efficiency in treating various tooth surfaces. Both types are essential tools in dental practices for maintaining oral health.</p></p>
