<p><ul><li>Manual System</li><li>Mechanical System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile Compactor Storage Systems are categorized into two main types: Manual Systems and Mechanical Systems. Manual Systems rely on human effort to operate sliding tracks or wheels, allowing users to compress storage space efficiently. In contrast, Mechanical Systems utilize motorized mechanisms to automate the movement of shelving units, offering enhanced speed and reduced labor. Both systems optimize storage capacity while maintaining accessibility, but Mechanical Systems typically provide greater efficiency and ease of use in larger or more demanding applications.</p></p>
