<p><ul><li>Cemented (Tungsten) Carbides</li><li>Cermets</li><li>Ceramics</li><li>cBN/PcBN</li><li>Diamond Tools</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indexable cutting tools market features various materials, each suited for specific machining applications. Cemented carbides, primarily tungsten carbide, offer high toughness and wear resistance, ideal for metalworking. Cermets combine ceramic and metal, enhancing wear resistance while maintaining toughness. Ceramics are used for high-speed applications due to their hardness. Cubic Boron Nitride (cBN) and PCBN excel in cutting hardened steels, while diamond tools are preferred for non-ferrous materials and composites, providing superior cutting performance and long tool life.</p></p>
