<p><p>The Ranging Optical Sensor Market is primarily divided into two types: Laser and LED. Laser sensors utilize focused light beams for precise distance measurement, offering high accuracy and long-range capabilities, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, LED sensors use broader light sources, providing shorter-range measurements with lower precision. While laser sensors excel in environments requiring exactitude, LED sensors are preferred for cost-effective solutions in applications where precision is less critical. Both types play vital roles in various industries.</p></p>
