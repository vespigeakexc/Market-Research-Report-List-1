<p><p>The commercial vehicle laminated glazing market is categorized into Class A and Class B types based on regulatory standards and applications. Class A laminated glazing is designed for windshields and front windows, prioritizing safety, visibility, and impact resistance. It typically meets stricter performance requirements. Class B laminated glazing, on the other hand, is used for side windows and rear applications, offering enhanced comfort and aesthetic appeal while still providing adequate safety features. Both classes contribute to vehicle structural integrity and occupant protection.</p></p>
