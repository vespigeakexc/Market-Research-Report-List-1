<p><p>Construction project management tools can be categorized into three primary market types: Installed-PC Software, Installed-Mobile Software, and Cloud-based Software. Installed-PC Software is locally hosted on computers, offering robust functionality but limited accessibility. Installed-Mobile Software is similar, designed for mobile devices, providing on-site access while still bound by local installation. In contrast, Cloud-based Software operates over the internet, enabling real-time collaboration, easy updates, and access from multiple devices, making it increasingly popular among construction professionals for its flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
